Join us at IEEE Smart World Congress 2026

We are delighted to invite you to the IEEE Smart World Congress 2026, a premier event that brings together thought leaders and researchers in smart technologies. The conference will be held in Rende (Calabria), Italy, from {{site.data.swc_data.dates.conferenceDates}}. IEEE SWC 2026 offers a unique platform for interdisciplinary collaboration across IoT, AI, smart cities, cybersecurity, and advanced computing. The program includes co-located conferences, keynote addresses, panels, workshops, and many networking opportunities — all set against Rende’s vibrant university and cultural scene.

The smart world is reshaping everyday life by combining sensing, communication, computation, and intelligence into our environments. These “smart things” promise huge societal benefits, but they also require fairness, transparency, and ethical stewardship. At IEEE SWC we celebrate innovation while encouraging research that is trustworthy and socially responsible. Join researchers, practitioners, and industry leaders at IEEE SWC 2026 to help shape the future of smart technologies.

Sincerely,
The IEEE SWC 2026 Organizing Committee

Venue & Location

Experience Italy’s Largest Residential Campus

IEEE SWC 2026 will take place at the University of Calabria (UNICAL) in Rende, Italy. The congress will be hosted inside the “Beniamino Andreatta” Convention Center, located at the heart of the iconic Ponte Pietro Bucci, a 1.3 km architectural landmark that seamlessly connects knowledge, culture, and nature.

Immerse yourself in an inspiring setting where academic excellence blends with Mediterranean landscapes and the rich cultural heritage of Calabria.
