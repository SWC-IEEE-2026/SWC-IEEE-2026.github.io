IEEE Smart World Congress 2026
Visa Information & Invitation Letters

Do you need a Visa?

Visas are required for some nationalities to travel to Italy for the conference. Please contact your travel agent and/or the Italian Consulate/Embassy in your country for the latest information on Italy visa requirements as soon as possible.

⚠️
Important: Visa processing times may vary. You are strongly advised to make your visa application at your earliest convenience if one is needed.

Invitation Letter Request

Do you need an invitation letter to the conference and an official acceptance notification for your paper?

Please Note: Invitation letters for VISA applications can be issued to registered participants only.

To issue your invitation letter / acceptance notification we need the following data provided clearly:

  • Last Name
  • First Name
  • Address
  • Affiliation
  • Nationality
  • Date Of Birth
  • Passport Number
  • Passport Date of Issue
  • Passport Date of Expiry
  • Passport Issuing Authority

How to Request

Please send the required information (clearly identified) to the General Chair to have your invitation letter / acceptance notification issued.
