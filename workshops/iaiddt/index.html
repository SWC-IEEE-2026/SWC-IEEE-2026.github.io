Join us at the IAIDDT Workshop 2025

The rapid evolution of Artificial Intelligence (AI), data analytics, and digital technologies is transforming how organizations operate, learn, and interact. This workshop, "Innovate: AI, Data & Digital Transformation," brings together researchers, educators, and industry practitioners to explore cutting-edge advancements and applications across sectors including education, management, finance, and healthcare. Topics will include AI-driven decision-making, generative AI for sustainable practices, digital human resource management, secure information systems, and the integration of metaverse and cognitive modeling in learning environments. By highlighting real-world implementations and data-driven strategies, the workshop aims to foster collaborative innovation and provide a platform for interdisciplinary dialogue on building smarter, more adaptive digital ecosystems. Participants will gain insights into the transformative potential of AI and data, and their role in shaping the future of digital society.

Discussion Topics

Topics of discussion may include, but are not limited to:

AI in Management and Organizational Efficiency
  • AI-driven digital HRM systems
  • Data analytics for decision-making
  • Smart enterprise resource planning
  • Generative AI for sustainable operations
Digital Transformation in Education
  • Metaverse and immersive learning technologies
  • Generative AI in curriculum development
  • AI-based assessment and mentoring platforms
  • Future-ready classrooms and digital pedagogy
Secure and Smart Information Systems
  • AI and ML for cybersecurity
  • Privacy in cloud and IoT environments
  • Securing educational and healthcare communication
  • Blockchain and secure data sharing
AI and Big Data in Finance and Healthcare
  • IT-enhanced performance metrics in financial institutions
  • CRM-based electronic health records
  • AI for sustainability in medical education
  • Financial risk prediction using machine learning
Social Media and Digital Marketing
  • AI-enhanced social media strategies
  • Consumer behavior analytics
  • Digital marketing in evolving markets
  • Influencer impact and sentiment analysis
Sustainable Innovation with AI and Data Analytics
  • Showcasing how AI supports sustainability goals, such as waste management and energy efficiency.

Submission Guidelines

We invite contributions in various forms: Regular paper (6 pages), Demo/Poster paper (2~4 pages). Papers must be prepared using IEEE Computer Society Proceedings manuscripts style. All submissions must be typeset in double-column IEEE format, with all fonts embedded. Submitted papers must include the contact information of all the authors. The IEEE LaTeX and Microsoft Word templates for conferences can be found at IEEE Manuscript Templates for Conference Proceedings at https://www.ieee.org/conferences/publishing/templates.html. The materials presented in the papers should not be published or under submission elsewhere. All papers should be submitted electronically via the link below. When submitting, please select the appropriate track for your paper.

Accepted and presented articles at IAIDDT 2025 will be included into the proceedings published by IEEE CPS (EI indexed). The IEEE Proceedings published by the IEEE CPS of the IEEE Computer Society will be indexed in ISI Clarivate Analytics' Web of Science, Scopus, EI Engineering Village, Google Scholar, ACM, CNKI, Scilit, DBLP, EBSCO, ProQuest and others.
